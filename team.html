About us

Who we are

erxes Inc - is the second generation of tech entrepreneurs in Mongolia. With over 8 years of experience, we are proud to be a part of the pioneer movement, to bring forth our country on the global stage, to hold the great responsibility of shaping the composition of the economy of Mongolia.

Mongolia, a country of untapped mineral wealth, has become a target for most advanced mining technology that however does little for indigenous people to speak out. At erxes, we work hard to prove that Mongolia can do better than trying to dig its way out. We believe in the untapped brilliant minds of young people who can start leading the way in the IT Industry on a global scale.

Our story

It can be daunting to live in a world where everything is shifting, especially the way we communicate. With apps like Facebook, Twitter, Whatsapp, WeChat and Instagram, consumers are communicating daily and on the go, more than ever. This means that businesses need to keep up. But it doesn’t have to be so hard.

erxes offers an all-in-one messaging platform for teams and individuals to take care of their customers, from leads, to engagement, and support. And the best part is that erxes is an Open (Source) platform.
